The Best Movies According to Carlos

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Natural Born Killers

The first time I watched it, I was 10 years old, and since then I can't get enough of it.

Apocalipsis Now

Aside from the atrocities of the Vietnam War, I was amazed at the idea of what we can become by pursuing an ideal of greatness or madness.

My Life as a Courgette

A hopeful movie that taught me that even if the world pushes you down, you can move forward.
